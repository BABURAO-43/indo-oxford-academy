About Us

Indo Oxford Academy is committed to holistic development. We focus on foundational literacy and numeracy in early years, scaffolded learning in middle school, and exam readiness for Class 10. Our teachers blend traditional pedagogy with interactive digital tools.

  • Environmental Studies
  • Art & Activity

Middle (Class 6 - 8)

Senior (Class 9 - 10)
